Leonhardt http://minoan.deaditerranean.com/linear-b-transliterations/pylos/py-e/py-ea/#comment-14010 Wed, 12 Dec 2012 04:52:22 +0000 http://minoan.deaditerranean.com/#comment-14010 The text is The Pylos Tablets: Texts of the the Inscriptions found 1939-1954, which was edited by Bennett in 1955. Leonard Palmer’s system of classification in his Interpretation of Mycenaean Greek Texts (1969 reprint) doesn’t include the E series, so I don’t know the classifications for Ea and Eq. Yet, Palmer correctly references this tablet as Eq 59. It’s possible that this tablet has since been reclassified, but I suspect a typographical error that has been compounded by repetition, so that, as late as 2011, Duhoux and Davies, in Vol 2 of A Companion to Linear B, list this tablet as Ea 59 rather than Eq 59. In some fonts, it’s difficult to distinguish the “a” from the “q” because the stem for the “q” is just slightly longer than that for the “a”. In the 1955 work, I had to look twice to ensure that I was reading “q” rather than “a”. Consequently, I was alert to the difference in your classification.
